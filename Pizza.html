pizza recipies

this is a recipe of pizza. its main ingredients are dough, chicken, olives, sauce and cheese


Ingredients


  • cheese
  • olives
  • bread
  • chicken
  • sauce
  • spices

Steps

  1. make the dough of the pizza
  2. cook the chicken
  3. apply the sauce to the dough
  4. put olives and other vegetables to the dough
  5. put chicken and cheese on the dough and bake the pizza at 180 degrees for 20 minutes
